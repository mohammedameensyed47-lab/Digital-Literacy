📘 Digital Literacy Project

📌 Overview

This project focuses on developing essential digital communication and online safety skills required for students in today’s digital world. It covers professional email writing, responsible social media use, and awareness of cybercrime along with preventive measures.

🎯 Objectives

To understand and apply proper email etiquette
To promote responsible use of social media
To create awareness about common cybercrimes
To learn practical ways to stay safe online

🛡️ Key Learnings

Importance of clear and professional communication
Risks associated with careless online behavior
Common cyber threats and how to prevent them
Safe practices for digital transactions

📎 Conclusion

This project highlights the importance of being responsible, aware, and cautious in the digital environment. By following proper communication practices and cybersecurity measures, students can protect themselves and use technology effectively.
